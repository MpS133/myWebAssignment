💡 Pro Tips

Add diagonal braces — triangles are the strongest shapes in engineering

Cables only pull, not push — pair them with rigid beams for support

Watch stress colors: green = safe · orange = stressed · red = breaking!

Use Road material for the deck where the car drives

Beams accumulate fatigue over time — build for endurance, not just the first second
